My Expertise

I’m a modern actuary with strong technical skills. I combine deep domain knowledge with modern tools and techniques to deliver value for the whole enterprise.

Ratemaking & Reserving

Crunching complex data to price risks precisely and building reliable models that forecast future claims are at the core of my work. I also leverage diagnostic tools to detect early warning signs when models begin to deviate.

Capital Management

I perform stress testing and extreme scenario modeling to optimize capital allocation, keeping it both efficient and robust. This area blends financial engineering with risk operations, all while satisfying complex regulatory standards.

Data Engineering

I code in Python and SQL, build scalable data pipelines, and deploy models that transform raw data into beautiful visuals that are easy to understand. I’m a full-stack actuary with data science and engineering knowledge.

While I’m fully employed, I occasionally take on one-off contracting projects. This is perfect for teams that need short-term support on actuarial insight, data-driven modeling, or a second set of eyes on complex risk problems.

My Experiences

With a strong foundation in pricing, reserving, and reinsurance, I’ve held key actuarial roles across high-growth and established companies. At my prior role as Lead Actuary at Cover, I led efforts in pricing strategy, product launches, and profitability analysis. At Uber, I focused on pricing and reserving for their rideshare and delivery businesses, its captive management, and worked on SOX compliance in preparation of its IPO over a span of more than three years. And lastly, my actuarial career began at Allstate, where I worked on rate indications, regulatory filings, and supported new product launches.
